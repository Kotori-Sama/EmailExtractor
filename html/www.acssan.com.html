American Cargoservice is a full service international and domestic freight forwarder. Since 1991, we have provided project logistics and transportation services for the alternative energy, mining, educational and manufacturing industries.

We partner with our clients and provide individualized service, to help our clients find solutions to their most complex logistics issues.

With over 10,000 member offices in 193 countries worldwide, our network of established logistics professionals work with us to find the fastest, most cost-efficient transit times by sea or air freight for standard, oversize, or overweight shipments. Standard and specialized trucking services are available for local and nationwide shipments.
