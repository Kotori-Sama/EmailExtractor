Guiding Your Next Step!

We Can Help

Learning disabilities & differences and/or emotional dysregulation challenges do not have to impede and define a student’s educational and social development. Let us help you find the best environment for your child's future.

The process

Discovery

Contact us and describe the issues with which your son or daughter is contending. We will recommend moving forward with a consultation or attempt to refer you to a more appropriate specialist.

Contact us

Initial Consultation

This is a ninety-minute meeting in which we discuss current evaluations, school reports, and other pertinent information. We will use all the information gathered to this point to design and present a strategy.  

Primary Placement

The first placement is the most appropriate environment for your son or daughter based on his or her current holistic presentation. We usher you through the admissions process and monitor progress throughout the first year. 

Secondary Placement

Sometimes it is necessary for the first placement to be a temporary school or program intended to prepare the student for an appropriate long-term school. We will identify the second placement as part of the initial strategy and begin the admissions process simultaneously with the first placement to optimize the transitions between placements.
