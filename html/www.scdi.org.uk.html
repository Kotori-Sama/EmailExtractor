SCDI has changed

Founded in 1931, SCDI was originally established as an independent membership network representing private, public and social economy sectors.

The fast-evolving changes in Scotland’s economy and society meant the time was right to modernise and re-energise.

From now on, SCDI will be known by its new name – Prosper.
