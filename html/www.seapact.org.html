Join us in building a sustainable future for the seafood industry.

​Sea Pact is a group of leading North American seafood companies dedicated to driving stewardship and continuous improvement of social, economic, and environmental responsibility throughout the global seafood supply chain.

Strategic Priorities

We accomplish our mission by focusing our collaborative efforts around three strategic priority areas:

Sector-based Sustainability

Responsible Aquaculture Education & Advocacy

Social Responsibility
