COMPANY PROFILE

MORRIS PRECISION TECHNOLOGY CO., LTD. has been dedicated to manufacturing industrial gas welding and cutting equipment since 1977. With solid teamwork and well-trained technicians, in 1997, we increased the professional medical oxygen therapy and suction devices to meet various market demands. Under the stress of tremendous sales growth, in 2015, we expanded to a brand new 25,000 sq. mt2 factory and increased CNC machines and test instruments to fulfill its ability of in-house production and the prospect of "made in Taiwan" only.
