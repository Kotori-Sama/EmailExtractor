Our services

Alliances & Public Affairs

We help our clients establish a dialogue with government, politics and other societal stakeholders which leads to regulations enabling sustainable innovations.

Communication & Design

With our unique mix of communication expertise and knowledge in the field of sustainability and health, we create appealing communication.

Compliance

We help companies comply with legislation and (international) guidelines in the areas of food safety, sustainability and claims.

Dialogue & Participation

We involve stakeholders in dialogue on societal & technological topics and organise the participation process for governments.

Digital

We combine knowledge with digital design and technical development to create apps, tools and digital platforms for a healthy world.

ESG & Sustainability

We help clients in the agri-food and health sector to develop an impactful and measurable strategy in the field of sustainability and health.
