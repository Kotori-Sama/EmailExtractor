Our Recipe: Family farm to Family Table
Since 1987, America’s Catch has been committed to providing the best quality, freshest catfish available on the market and has continued to serve up catfish nationwide. We are a vertically integrated catfish processor and the largest catfish producer in the U.S. We control the entire production process from the feed mill to the hatchery to delivery. And, America's Catch is the only pre-chilled live fish plant in the U.S. This allows us to maintain the highest quality standards assuring that the freshest catfish reach the marketplace. At America’s Catch we are committed to providing our customers the finest quality products available when you want it, where you want it, and the way you want it. We are America's Gourmet Catfish Company, the tastiest, freshest catfish produced!
